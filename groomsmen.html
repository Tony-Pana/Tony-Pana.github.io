Meet The Groom Party



Dave Sukharan

We barely knew about each other in middle school, Brooklyn Junior High, but really hung out during freshman year of college @ the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities after his mom gave me a ride to orientation (thanks to someone bailing on me as a ride). Dave and his family are integral to my success during and after college, and his family also makes some bomb @$$ food. We have done many crazy things together such as performing in at events like the time when we taught dance to a crew, MacGyver'ing poor man solutions like the time when we had to travel a mile to do baskets of laundry on a computer chair, and of course, met a lot of amazing people along the way. The one thing that I love about Dave is his ability to make anyone feel warm and welcomed, and did I mention he is like the best speech giver ever?



Peter Dang

My favorite memory was convincing a large group of people to pretend that they forgot his birthday and did not care. Then later in the week, we surprised him with a birthday party, showed an amazing dance video and showered him with lots of love. The one thing that I love about Peter is his ability to go the extra mile to help anyone for any reason, and did I mention he was a lapras with big muscles?
